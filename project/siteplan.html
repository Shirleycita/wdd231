Site Plan

Reason of the name

I chose the name "Slowly But Shirley" for my website for 2 reasons: 1. I was inspired by the quote "slowly but surely" and since my name rhymes with surely, I thought it's the perfect name for my website and 2. Because of the meaning of the quote, it will take time to learn because it requires practice, watch makeup tutorials, you will learn step by step everything you need to know about beauty and doing your own makeup which is a slow process and you need to be patient but at the end of the day you will succeed.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to help everyone who is interested in makeup but has no idea on what to do or what they need to start doing their own makeup. This site is for everyone who wants to learn how to do a natural, dramatic or colorful makeup look. Here you will find makeup tutorials, guides on what makeup products you need, makeup recommendations from affordable to high-end makeup products, and where to buy them.

Scenarios

Scenario #1

Question: "How do visitors know how well the product will perform?"
Content Focus: Using and showing the recommended product in the videos,
doing a list with pros and cons depending on what type of skin they have and also, reviews from people that have used that specific product and how well it performed. Comparing quality and prices.

Scenario #2

Question: "What type of video content will help someone who is a beginner in makeup?"
Content Focus: Detailed videos with step by step on how to do your eyebrows, what makeup brushes are used for, how to find your perfect foundation and concealer shade as well as how to apply them, how to use liquid eyeliner depending on your eye shape, how to prepare your skin before applying makeup, and different makeup looks to follow along.
